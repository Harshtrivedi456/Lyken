Plot Summary
The season shifts from Hyosan High to Seoul, exploring the spread of the zombie virus beyond the school.  Nam On-jo, now a university student, must confront trauma and new threats while leading a group of survivors in a city under siege. The story emphasizes evolving survival tactics, deeper character arcs, and the psychological toll of the apocalypse. 

Fans can expect a darker, more intense season with higher stakes, improved effects, and the return of beloved characters alongside fresh faces from hit series like Squid Game and Weak Hero: Class 2.
